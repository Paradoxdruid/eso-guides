## Beam DPS Quickstart Guide (Update 49)

There's lots of ways to do good damage. But you want a cheatsheet on something effective, so you can get back to playing?

### The Basics

- **Race**: ![dark elf](./assets/icons/dark_elf.png){: class="inline-image"} Dark Elf, ![orc](./assets/icons/orc.png){: class="inline-image"} Orc, ![khajiit](./assets/icons/khajiit.png){: class="inline-image"} Khajiit are the best races for DPS. Unsure? Pick Dark Elf.

- **Mundus**: ![thief mundus](./assets/icons/mundusstone-Thief.png){: class="inline-image"} [The Thief](<https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:The_Thief_(Mundus_Stone)>) provides important crit chance

- **Attributes**: 64 points in ![deni](./assets/icons/runestone-Deni.png){: class="inline-image"} **Stamina**<br>(damage is based off your highest stat, so you want all points in one category; high stamina is also important for trial mechanics that involve blocking, roll-dodging, or breaking free)

- **Food**: Max Stamina/Stamina Recovery/Health food (![dubious](./assets/icons/food-Dubious_Camoran_Throne.png){: class="inline-image"} [Dubious Camoran Throne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Dubious_Camoran_Throne) or ![artaeum](./assets/icons/food-Brown_Chunky_Soup.png){: class="inline-image"} [Artaeum Takeaway Broth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Artaeum_Takeaway_Broth)) is best for content. For parsing on the trial dummy, use Max Stamina/Stamina Recovery food (![lavafoot](./assets/icons/food-Lava_Foot_Soup-And-Saltrice.png){: class="inline-image"} [Lava Foot Soup and Saltrice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Lava_Foot_Soup-and-Saltrice))

- **Subclassing**: Having useful skill lines from multiple classes greatly enhances your damage potential. Here, we'll use three all-around useful skill lines:
  - **REQUIRED**: ![arcanist](./assets/icons/gp_class_arcanist.png){: class="inline-image"} Arcanist: Herald of the Tome ![herald](./assets/icons/ability_arcanist_002_b.png){: class="inline-image"}
  - **REQUIRED**: ![nb](./assets/icons/gp_class_nightblade.png){: class="inline-image"} Nightblade: Assassination ![assassin](./assets/icons/ability_nightblade_007_b.png){: class="inline-image"}
  - **PICK ONE**:
    - ![necro](./assets/icons/gp_class_necromancer.png){: class="inline-image"} Necromancer: Gravelord ![grave](./assets/icons/ability_necromancer_002_b.png){: class="inline-image"}
    - ![plar](./assets/icons/gp_class_templar.png){: class="inline-image"} Templar: Dawn's Wrath ![dawn](./assets/icons/ability_templar_under_exposure.png){: class="inline-image"}
    - ![sorc](./assets/icons/gp_class_sorcerer.png){: class="inline-image"} Sorcerer: Storm Calling ![storm](./assets/icons/ability_sorcerer_thundering_presence.png){: class="inline-image"}

### Gear

- The most versatile starting gear is:
  - 1 piece ![slimecraw](./assets/icons/armor-Head-Slimecraw.png){: class="inline-image"} **Slimecraw** monster set (or any +crit chance monster piece). Usually in Light armor weight, for added penetration.
  - ![velothi](./assets/icons/armor-Velothi_Ur-Mage's_Amulet.png){: class="inline-image"} **Velothi Ur-Mage's Amulet**
  - 5 piece ![deadly](./assets/icons/deadly_strike.png){: class="inline-image"} **Deadly Strike** (buy from guild traders or farm PvP) on the front bar (2 daggers)
  - 5 piece ![ansuuls](./assets/icons/ansuuls_torment.png){: class="inline-image"} **Ansuul's Torment** on the body/rings
  - a ![staff](./assets/icons/maelstrom_staff.png){: class="inline-image"} **Maelstrom Inferno Staff** backbar (for single target-fights ![greatsword](./assets/icons/weapon-Greatsword-Maelstrom.png){: class="inline-image"} **Maelstrom Greatsword** )
  - (For purely single target fights, ![null arca](./assets/icons/null_arca.png){: class="inline-image"} **Null Arca** with ![tideborn](./assets/icons/tideborn.png){: class="inline-image"} **Tide-Born Wildstalker** is stronger)
- At the beginning, craftable ![wrath](./assets/icons/deadly_strike.png){: class="inline-image"} **Order's Wrath** or ![tideborn](./assets/icons/tideborn.png){: class="inline-image"} **Tide-Born Wildstalker** can replace Ansuul's Torment.
- Enchantments:
  - all body pieces Divine trait with +Stam glyphs
  - all jewelry Bloodthirsty with +Physical Harm glyphs
  - daggers Nirnhoned/Charged, one with Flame glyph one with Poison glyph. Nirnhoned/Precise is better in AoE-heavy fights.

### What to do

- Arcanists have long range and high cleave; always aim your beam to maximize targets hit. It also strikes targets immediately behind you, so you can press up against them and damage them as well ("butt beam")
- Always get to 3 crux before beaming. With Inspired Scholarship running, that means 2 casts of Flail, then beam. **Beam is over 50% of your damage. ABB. Always Be Beaming.**
- Arcanists cannot block-cast well; both Flail and Beam can't be block cast. In trial mechanics that require block casting, be mindful of this fact.
